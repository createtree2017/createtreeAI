Please update the UI design to align with the following detailed style guide, ensuring a cohesive and modern aesthetic suitable for a maternity lifestyle service targeting women in their 20s and 30s:

1. **Color Palette**:
   - Utilize a harmonious blend of soft and warm colors to evoke a sense of comfort and trust.
   - Suggested colors include:
     - Lavender (#E6E6FA)
     - Mint Green (#98FF98)
     - Peach (#FFE5B4)
     - Beige (#F5F5DC)
   - Ensure sufficient contrast between text and background for readability.

2. **Typography**:
   - Primary Font: Noto Sans KR for Korean text to maintain readability and a clean appearance.
   - Secondary Font: Poppins for English text to provide a modern and friendly look.
   - Maintain consistent font sizes and weights to establish a clear hierarchy.

3. **Iconography**:
   - Implement simple line icons that are intuitive and consistent throughout the interface.
   - Icons should be easily recognizable and support the accompanying text.

4. **Buttons**:
   - Design rounded buttons with subtle shadow effects to enhance clickability and visual appeal.
   - Use color variations to indicate different button states (e.g., hover, active).

5. **Layout**:
   - Adopt a card-based layout to clearly delineate different sections and improve content organization.
   - Ensure adequate spacing between elements to prevent a cluttered appearance.
   - Utilize a responsive grid system to maintain consistency across various screen sizes.

6. **Imagery**:
   - Incorporate soft, emotional visuals that resonate with the target audience.
   - Images should reflect themes of motherhood, care, and community.
   - Ensure images are optimized for fast loading without compromising quality.

7. **Navigation**:
   - Implement a sticky top navigation bar that remains visible as users scroll.
   - Include clear labels and intuitive icons for each menu item.
   - Ensure the navigation is responsive and functions seamlessly on mobile devices.

8. **Accessibility**:
   - Ensure all text has sufficient contrast against its background.
   - Provide alternative text for all images.
   - Design interactive elements to be easily navigable via keyboard.
